Making a mold

The first process was making a paper mold to cut the shape of a ring from a silver plate. After cutting out the shape, I annealed and bent the silver piece.

Soldering

I made a top of part and body part separately, and then I put them together by soldering. I needed to think about the placement carefully to match the flower pattern to the body part. After soldering all of the parts, I cut the extra.

About

These art pieces were created as part of a school project and my private projects. The course I took was ART 106J Jewelry/Sculpture at Kapiolani Community College.

Custom design Brochure for a Unique Artist

In this project, I designed a brochure for an exhibition of Kohei Nawa at the Smithsonian Art Museum. Kohei Nawa is one of Japanâ€™s new generation of enthusiastic young artists. He has held solo exhibitions at various museums in Japan, the Search Gallery in London, the Joan Miro Foundation in Barcelona.

Pixcell Dear Collections

He thought that seeing the objects inside through a lot of spherical lenses like pixels would give people different perspectives and feelings. He tried to find a new concept of beauty using a fusion of modern technology and nature.

Brochure Design

I tried to make a simple and stylish design without using colors so that his novel and unique sculpture would stand out. I chose "Cronos Pro" for the body type and "Centennial LTStd" for the general information.

About

This project was created as part of a school project. The course was ART 120 Intro to Typography and the assignment was to create an art exhibition brochure for Kohei Nawa.

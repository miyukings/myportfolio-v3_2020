Magazine Designs

Custom Design for a Travel and Art Magazine

Through the process of designing a publication, I learned that choosing appropriate typefaces, imagery, and creating dynamic compositions is crucial.

Travel Magazine

In this project, I planned an art-related travel magazine featuring the architectures of Le Corbusier, which I have admired. I chose a beautiful image and a stylish typeface for the front cover to attract readers to want to explore a new place.

Feature Page of Le Corbusier

His masterpiece, the Longchamp Chapel, is a simple and uniquely shaped church, and on the feature page, I chose Futura for the header to match the dynamic image. I tried to create a classical and elegant atmosphere in consideration of the historical background using Electra for the body.

Table of Contents

On the table of contents page, I placed beautifully colored images and text to attract readers and guide the flow line. I spent most of the time to figure out the appropriate balance of placement between imagery and text.

About

This project was created as part of a school project. The course was ART 120 Intro to Typography and the assignment was to create custom Magazine of Art and Travel.

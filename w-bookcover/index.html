Radiolab Book Cover Designs

Custom Illustrations for a Unique Author

Radiolab is a New York-based famous radio program discus about several kinds of interesting topics hosted by Jad Abumrad and Robert Krulwich. The objective of this project was to create book jackets for three podscast as a  trilogy.

Proposed Designs

In this exercise, I drew more than 100 sketches for each topic and created three designs based on those sketches. Once the design direction was selected, I created the remaining two designs, which were cohesive and polished.

About

This project was created as part of a school project. The course was ART 120 Intro to Typography and the assignment was to create custom book covers for David Sedaris.
